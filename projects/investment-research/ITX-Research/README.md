# Inditex (ITX) Equity Research

Equity research project analyzing Inditex business model, competitive positioning, and valuation.

## Project overview
Comprehensive analysis of Inditex, one of the world's largest fashion retailers, focusing on operational efficiency, growth drivers, and financial performance.

## Methodology
- Business model and competitive analysis
- Industry and market positioning review
- Financial statement analysis
- Valuation using comparable companies and intrinsic valuation methods

## Objective
Evaluate Inditex’s investment attractiveness and identify key drivers of future performance.

## Files included
- ITX Equity Research report
- Financial analysis and valuation materials
